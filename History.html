HISTORY OF THE CARTOON

The first animated television series was Crusader Rabbit. Animated sitcoms first appeared in the 1960s, with The Flintstones (1960–1966), followed by The Jetsons (1962–1987).
French cartoonist and animator Émile Cohl is often referred to as "the father of the animated cartoon. He created the first animated film using what came to be known as traditional animation methods: the 1908 Fantasmagorie.
